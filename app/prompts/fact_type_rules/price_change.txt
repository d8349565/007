Additional rules for PRICE_CHANGE:
- product_name goes into qualifiers.product_name if stated.
- price_type goes into qualifiers: list_price, market_price, contract_price, average_price.
- change_pct goes into qualifiers if a percentage change is stated.
- reason goes into qualifiers if the evidence explains why the price changed.
- Distinguish between: price increase (涨价/上调), price decrease (降价/下调), price adjustment (调价).
- predicate options: price_increased, price_decreased, price_adjusted, price_in_period.
- If the evidence gives both old and new prices, extract the current/new price as value_num.
- segment goes into qualifiers if stated.
- Do not confuse cost changes with price changes.
