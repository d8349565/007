Additional rules for SALES_VOLUME:

## CRITICAL: Industry scope restriction
- Only extract SALES_VOLUME for the **coatings/paints/resin/pigment** industry and directly related chemical products.
- Do NOT extract as SALES_VOLUME:
  - Upstream industry data: 造船完工量, 造船订单量, 手持订单量, 港口加注量 → these provide context but are NOT coatings industry facts
  - Trade show statistics: 展览面积, 参展单位数量, 参观人数 → these describe events, not product volume
  - General economic indicators unrelated to coatings
- If the subject is a shipbuilding/造船 metric or a port/港口 metric, do NOT extract it as SALES_VOLUME even if it has a numeric value with physical units.

## Field rules:
- metric_name (e.g. 总产量, 销量, 表观消费量, 出货量) must go into qualifiers.metric_name.
- yoy and qoq percentages must go into qualifiers.yoy or qualifiers.qoq, NOT into value_num.
- value_num should be the absolute volume number.
- unit is typically 万吨, 吨, 万升, etc.
- If multiple volume metrics appear in one evidence (e.g. 产量 and 消费量), extract each as a separate record.
- scope (e.g. 全国, 某地区) goes into qualifiers.scope if stated.
- Do not confuse output (产量) with sales (销量) with consumption (消费量).
- If the evidence mentions a time period (e.g. "2024年", "今年"), always extract it as time_expr.
