Additional rules for COOPERATION:
- Both parties (subject and object) must be clearly identified from the evidence.
- cooperation_type goes into qualifiers: strategic_cooperation, joint_development, supply_agreement, framework_agreement.
- Distinguish between: "达成合作意向" (intention) vs "已签约" (signed) vs "正式启动" (started).
- scope goes into qualifiers if stated (e.g. 新能源汽车涂层联合开发).
- duration goes into qualifiers if stated.
- Do not exaggerate the cooperation strength beyond what the evidence states.
- Supply relationships (供应关系) and general cooperation (合作) should use different predicates.
