Additional rules for CAPACITY:
- Distinguish between: designed capacity (设计产能), actual capacity (实际产能), planned capacity (规划产能).
- product_type should go into qualifiers.product_type if stated.
- is_planned should be true in qualifiers if the capacity is planned/future, not yet achieved.
- phase (e.g. 一期, 二期) goes into qualifiers.phase.
- Do not confuse capacity (产能) with actual output (产量). Capacity is potential; output is realized.
- If expansion involves capacity, it may be both EXPANSION and CAPACITY facts — extract each separately.
