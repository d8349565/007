Additional rules for MARKET_SHARE:
- market_scope MUST be present in qualifiers when extracting a share percentage. If the evidence does not state the market scope, do NOT extract this fact.
- Distinguish between single-company share and combined share (e.g. CR3/CR5/CR10/top10).
- predicate must be a Chinese verb phrase. Available options: 市场份额为、占市场份额、占全国市场份额、市占率为、市场份额减少、前十强市占率、前五市占率 (choose the most appropriate Chinese phrase).
- If the text says "前十强企业市占率96%", subject should be "前十强企业" (group), not a single company.
- ranking_scope goes into qualifiers (e.g. top10, top5).
- source_scope goes into qualifiers if stated (e.g. 按产值, 按产量).
- unit for share is always "%".
